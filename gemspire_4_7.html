FULL RULES ▾
Special gems. A line of 4 forges a Striped gem that clears a row or column; an L or T forges an Area gem that bursts a 3×3; a line of 5 forges a Colour Bomb — swap it onto any gem to detonate every gem of that colour. Detonating a special feeds you: stripes surge energy, area gems grant Block, colour bombs supercharge. Forging one briefly stills the clock. Keep matching a colour and it levels up, banking more energy per match. Wild gems count as any colour.

Focus. The blue FOCUS meter fills as you match, cascade and forge. When full, tap it (or press F) to freeze the enemy clock — a beat to read the board and plan, without losing the real-time pressure the rest of the time.

The foes. Each presses one system: the Gravelock turns gems to stone, the Tithe-Collector doubles card costs, the Quickening hastens its clock and strikes twice, the Rot-Witch sows creeping rot and weakens you, and the Chromavore locks a colour you can't match. The Prism Tyrant wields them all. Every threat is announced on the INCOMING bar before it lands. Beat a foe and its spoils favour the very tools that answer it.

The climb. Your five cards are drawn from a deck — play one and draw the next. Climb three acts (the Quarry, the Treasury, the Clockwork), each sealed by a boss, with the Prism Tyrant at the summit. Route a branching path: take relics and cards from the spoils, bank gold for the Peddler, gamble at events, and pause at Rest sites to heal or hone a card. HP carries between fights; energy mostly resets.

GEMSPIRE

Swipe a gem toward a neighbour (or tap two) to make lines of 3 or more. Each match banks colored energy. Spend energy on the cards below to strike or defend.

Match bigger to forge special gems: a line of 4 makes a Striped gem that clears a whole row or column; an L or T makes an Area gem that bursts a 3×3; a line of 5 makes a Colour Bomb — swap it onto any gem to detonate every gem of that colour. Keep matching a colour and it levels up, banking more energy per match. Wild gems count as any colour.

A roster of foes awaits, each pressing one system: the Gravelock turns gems to stone, the Tithe-Collector doubles card costs, the Quickening hastens its clock and strikes twice, the Rot-Witch sows creeping rot and weakens you, and the Chromavore locks a colour you can\u2019t match. The Prism Tyrant wields them all. Every threat is announced on the INCOMING bar before it lands \u2014 read it, then match fast.

Your five cards are drawn from a deck: play one and you immediately draw the next. You\u2019ll climb three acts \u2014 the Quarry, the Treasury, the Clockwork \u2014 each themed on one seam and sealed by its own boss, with the Prism Tyrant waiting at the summit. Route a branching path: take relics and cards from the spoils, bank gold to spend at the Peddler on cards, a relic, or thinning your deck, gamble at risk-and-reward events, and pause at Rest sites to heal or hone a card to its \uFF0B version. Your HP carries between fights; energy mostly resets.
